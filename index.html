About Us

The Environment Protection and Awareness Club of BIT Mesra, popularly known as EPAC is a club whose sole motive to instigate the students of our campus to proactively contemplate about the detrimental effects on our environment in the current scenario. It has been observed that rapid urbanization, and industrialization has led to a serious global environment crisis. We, as a student body aim at, improvising the exisiting systems which lay in the favour of nature. We have started a plethora of initiatives in our lush green Institute campus to aid the upliftment of the environment and make the students aware of the repercussions of an unhealthy environment. With an objective to nurture nature in return to what all it has done for us, we selflessly carry out activities with paramount dedication and unequivocal semblance of ideas to save what has been ours for so many years. We work in consonance with the latest advancements in technology to bring about small differences which might lead towards a huge change.

Our Events

April 22nd, 1970 was a prominent day for the United States of America, as it went down in history as the inception of Earth Day, the nation-wide initiative for protecting the environment. Decades passed by, and the Earth Day was upscaled as a world-wide initiative, and thus including more than 192 countries of the world.
The 51st commemoration of Earth Day is themed on "Restore Our Earth" and its focus is on natural processes, emerging green technologies and innovative thinking that can restore the world’s ecosystems. It also features five primary programmes: The Canopy Project, Food and Environment, Climate Literacy, the Global Earth Challenge, and The Great Global CleanUp.

The combined effects caused by amazon forest fires are irreversible and imposes great threat to us and future generations as well. The explication of what we're losing in these fires is perhaps ineffable for we are losing the so called 'LUNGS' of the Earth.

With the aim to find a solution for the different environmental crisis of this era, we at EPAC, BIT Mesra introduce you to “Beyond Crisis", an event where your awareness and analytical skills are put to a test.
